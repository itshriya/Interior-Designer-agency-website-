About Livora

Design is not just what it looks like, it's how it feels.

Founded in 2011, Livora emerged from a passion for architecture and deep appreciation for spatial harmony. We don't just decorate rooms; we curate experiences that resonate with the people who inhabit them.

Our approach is deeply collaborative. We listen intimately to our clients' needs, blending their vision with our expertise to create spaces that are structurally sound, aesthetically magnificent, and incredibly comfortable.
